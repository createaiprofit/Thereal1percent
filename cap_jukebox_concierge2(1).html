Concierge Host Terms & Conditions
01 Host Agreement & Commission Structure ▾
By applying as a CAP Concierge Host, you agree to list your property exclusively through the Create AI Profit concierge booking system for the duration of your agreement. CAP retains a 15% commission on all bookings processed through the platform. Hosts receive 85% of net booking revenue, paid weekly to their in-app wallet. The $500 signup bonus is issued upon completion of your first confirmed booking and is non-transferable.
02 Property Standards & Compliance ▾
All properties listed under CAP Concierge must meet minimum hospitality standards: clean, photo-verified, and compliant with local short-term rental regulations. CAP reserves the right to inspect listings, request updated photos, or remove non-compliant properties at any time without notice. Hosts are solely responsible for obtaining all required local permits, licenses, and insurance. CAP is not liable for any regulatory fines, guest incidents, or property damage.
03 Guest Relations & Conduct ▾
Hosts agree to maintain a minimum 4.5-star guest rating. Properties falling below this threshold for two consecutive months may be suspended from the platform. All guest communications must be conducted through the CAP concierge system. Direct solicitation of guests outside the platform is prohibited and will result in immediate termination and forfeiture of pending earnings. CAP members receive priority booking access as part of their 1% Playground membership.
04 Revenue, Payments & Wallet ▾
All host earnings are deposited into the CAP in-app wallet within 48 hours of guest checkout confirmation. Hosts may request advanced access of up to 50% of their pending balance at any time — interest-free, auto-repaid from future deposits. CreateAIProfit.com retains ownership of all wallet infrastructure. Withdrawals to external bank accounts are processed weekly on Fridays. A 2.5% processing fee applies to all external transfers. Tax compliance is the sole responsibility of the host.
05 Arbitrage Program ▾
CAP offers a sublease arbitrage program for hosts who do not own property. Under this program, CAP assists in identifying and negotiating master lease agreements with property owners. Participants in the arbitrage program agree to a 20% revenue share with CAP (vs. 15% for property owners). CAP provides training, templates, and ongoing support. Participants are responsible for all lease obligations, utilities, and furnishing costs. CAP does not guarantee income or occupancy rates.
06 Termination & Dispute Resolution ▾
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Immediate termination without notice applies in cases of fraud, platform abuse, or violation of guest conduct policies. All disputes are subject to mandatory arbitration in Nevada, USA, under AAA rules. No class actions. No jury trials.
